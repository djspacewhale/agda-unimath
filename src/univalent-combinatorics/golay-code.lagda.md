## Idea

The **Golay code** is an especially nice 11-dimensional subspace of the
23-dimensional vector space over the finite field with two elements. It and an
extension of it are important in the theory of finite simple groups and in
defining the [Mathieu groups](finite-group-theory.mathieu-groups.md).
